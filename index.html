Our Technology

Advanced Code Reasoning with Generative AI

Our AI models understand, analyze, and generate complex code by reasoning about its logic and behavior. It helps developers debug, optimize, and refactor code more efficiently, bridging the gap between natural language specifications and executable code. Our technology enhances productivity and reduces errors in software development.

Contact Us to learn more

Contextual Understanding

Our systems are capable of understanding diverse complex codebases, and can grasp the deeper intent behind code logic, handle ambiguity or poor documentation.

Accurate Inferencing

Our systems make accurate inferences when interacting with external systems or dynamic environments, limiting its ability to offer precise solutions or detect subtle bugs.

Cross-Module Reasoning

Our system can capture Asynchronous communications between microservices, and can handle Dynamic dependency injection, Runtime configuration changes, Cross-language interfaces as well as Implicit state sharing.

Safety and Reliability

Our system combines advanced analysis techniques like static analysis, symbolic execution, and formal verification to ensure the safety, reliability, and compliance of mission-critical code. It monitors performance, detects anomalies, and guarantees fault tolerance and recovery for high-reliability systems.

Security

Our system implements robust safeguards against vulnerabilities and attacks, ensuring data integrity and preventing unauthorized access. It incorporates secure coding, continuous monitoring, and real-time threat detection to maintain the mission critical system's reliability and safety under all conditions.

Continuous Innovations

Our code reasoning system is designed to learn and adapt, enabling capabilities such as guaranteeing response times for critical operations, proving resource consumption bounds, and verifying failure recovery paths. Through continuous runtime verification and early detection of potential failures, the system will evolve to proactively ensure system reliability and performance, anticipating and addressing issues before they impact mission-critical operations.
